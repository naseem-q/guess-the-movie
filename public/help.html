THE ULTIMATE PARTY GAME

Guess • Compete • Win

🎮 What is this game?
!
A multiplayer party game where everyone plays from their phones. A blurred image slowly reveals on a shared TV screen — guess what it is before your friends do! The faster you answer, the more points you earn.
🚀 Start a game
1
Create a game
Open the app on your phone. Tap "Create Game", enter your name, pick a category and difficulty, then tap "Create Room".
2
Connect the TV
Open the app on the TV browser. Tap "Display on TV" and enter the room code shown on your phone. The TV is now your game screen.
3
Invite friends
Friends open the app on their phones, tap "Join Game", enter the same room code + their name. Everyone appears in the lobby.
4
Start playing
The game master taps "Start Game". The TV shows the image, players answer from their phones. 5 rounds, 10 questions each!
⭐ Scoring
⚡
Speed matters! The faster you answer correctly, the more points you get — up to 1,000 points per question.
❌
Wrong answers give 0 points — but there's no penalty, so guess fast!
⏱️
If time runs out before you answer, you get 0 points for that question.
📺 Connect to a TV
A
Smart TV browser
Open the website on your Smart TV's browser, tap "Display on TV", enter the room code.
B
Laptop + HDMI
Open the website on a laptop, connect the laptop to the TV with an HDMI cable, then tap "Display on TV".
C
Cast / AirPlay
Open the website on your phone or laptop, then cast the browser tab to your TV using Chromecast or AirPlay.
💡
Tap the ⛶ fullscreen button on the TV page for the best experience — hides the browser bar completely.
📲 Install the app

Install the game as an app on your phone for quick access — no app store needed!

1
Open the website in Chrome.
2
You should see an "Install" banner at the bottom — tap Install.
3
If no banner appears: tap the ⋮ menu (top right) → "Add to Home Screen" or "Install App".
✅
The app icon will appear on your home screen. It opens in fullscreen — no browser bar!
1
Open the website in Safari (not Chrome).
2
Tap the Share button ↗ (square with arrow, bottom center).
3
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen".
4
Tap "Add" in the top right corner.
⚠️
This only works in Safari. Chrome on iPhone doesn't support home screen apps properly.
1
Open the website in Chrome or Edge.
2
Click the install icon in the address bar (⊕ or ↓), or go to ⋮ menu → "Install App".
✅
The app will open as its own window — great for the TV display!
👑 Game master controls
⏭
Skip — skip a broken or too-hard question.
⏸
Pause — freeze the timer. Tap again to resume.
🛑
End Game — jump to final results immediately.
▶
Next — appears after each answer reveal. Tap to move on.
❓ FAQ
📶
Do all phones need to be on the same WiFi? No! The game runs online — everyone just needs internet access.
👥
How many players? 2 to 8 players per game.
🔇
Can I mute the music? Yes — tap the 🔊 button on the TV screen.
📱
Can the game master play too? Yes! The creator plays from their phone like everyone else.
